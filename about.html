About Us

Western Michigan University Bookstore

Western Michigan University aims to ease student's search for their course materials at affordable prices. We have been providing new as well as used hardcopy and digital textbooks to students of all years here at Western. We also provide the option of renting out books at almost half the price of a new text book. Further, Western students are provided with student's discounts so that concern for expense doesn't appear become concern for education. Happy Studying!
